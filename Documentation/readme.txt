Simple Newsbars
Add three "MyBB Community like" newsbars to your Portal and Index

A rather simple plugin to create 3 "MyBB like" newsbars on your board with the option to display on Index and or Portal. Note you can disable any or all of the news bars and you can opt to display on Index or Portal or not....

To Install:

Upload The Files, And Go to Admin CP And Activate!
